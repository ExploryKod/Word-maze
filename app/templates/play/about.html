LexiLoop Labs

We design playful puzzles and word experiences that turn curiosity into small victories—one maze, one riddle, and one grin at a time.

Our story

LexiLoop Labs began as a late-night side project between two linguistics students and a frontend developer who insisted “games should teach without feeling like homework.” Today we are a tiny, entirely fictional crew of twelve spread across imaginary time zones—from Nova Reykjavik to the floating co-working barge moored off Lisbon (again: fictional).

We publish experiments under playful codenames, partner with indie educators, and occasionally ship a polished title when the stars align and the coffee holds out.

At a glance

  • Focus: narrative word games & vocabulary play
  • HQ: The Rotating Zeppelin Annex, suite 404 (fictional)
  • Principle: no dark patterns, no shame—just clever loops
“If a player closes the tab smiling, we have done our job—even if they did not beat the level.”

— Mira V., Lead Narrative Designer (fictional)

What we optimize for

Clarity

Rules you can explain in one breath. Interfaces that stay out of the way until you need them.

Respect

Fictional or not, we pretend our players have jobs, kids, and limited patience—so we never waste their time.

Delight

Micro-animations, satisfying sounds, and the occasional absurd Easter egg—because joy is part of learning.
